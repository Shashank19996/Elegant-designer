About Us

Founded in 2024 by Shashank Shrivastava and Goldi Shrivastava, Elegant Designer has established itself as a premier interior design firm known for creating sophisticated, functional, and personalized spaces. Our team of passionate designers combines creativity with technical expertise to deliver exceptional results for every client.

We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone and that your space should tell your unique story. Whether we're designing a cozy family home or a dynamic corporate office, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and attention to detail.

Our collaborative process ensures that your vision is at the heart of every decision we make. From concept to completion, we're committed to exceeding your expectations and creating spaces that inspire and delight.
